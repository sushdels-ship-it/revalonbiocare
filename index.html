Esogred DSR

Esomeprazole 40mg & Domperidone 30mg

A systematic review and meta-analysis of randomized controlled trials indicated that combining domperidone with proton pump inhibitors (PPIs) like esomeprazole improved GERD symptoms more effectively than PPIs alone.

Fluqik

Oseltamivir 75mg

Influenza Treatment: A meta-analysis involving over 100,000 patients demonstrated that oseltamivir significantly reduced the duration of fever and influenza-like symptoms. It also decreased hospitalization rates and the need for antibiotics.

Argivon

L-Arginine 5gm with Zinc & Folic Acid

Erectile Dysfunction: A multicenter, double-blind, randomized, placebo-controlled trial demonstrated that high-dose L-Arginine supplementation improved erectile function in men with vasculogenic erectile dysfunction. Blood Pressure: L-Arginine supplementation has been associated with reductions in blood pressure, particularly in individuals with hypertension.

Luprest

Dydrogesterone 10mg

Endometriosis: Studies have shown that dydrogesterone significantly reduces dysmenorrhea and pelvic pain in women with endometriosis. Threatened Miscarriage: A systematic review indicated that dydrogesterone reduces the odds of miscarriage in women experiencing threatened abortion.
